The Shopping Club consists of 3 Apps: Companies management, Cards management and Club Store. These 3 apps work together allowing companies to register their colaborators to get points to buy products at the club store.

My team developed the Club Store:

Features:

  • Products are displayed with price/ discount specific to current user's company discount;
  • Client area with: "My account", "My orders", "My addresses", "Extract (points)", "My info", "My favourites"
  • Shopping Cart;
  • Favorite products;
  • API comunication with cards app and companies app;
